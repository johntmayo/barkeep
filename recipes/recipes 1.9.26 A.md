## **Bay Leaf Daiquiri**

*Kathryn “Pepper” Stashek — Bar Kabawa, New York City*

**Ingredients (1 serving):**

* 2 oz lightly aged blended rum (e.g., Chairman’s Reserve White)  
* ¾ oz lime juice blend (1:3 key lime : Persian lime)  
* ¾ oz bay leaf syrup *(see below)*  
* 8 g fresh bay leaves  
* 4 drops saline solution *(see below)*  
* Liquid nitrogen, for nitro-muddling

**Directions:**

1. Add bay leaves to a shaker.  
2. Pour a small amount of liquid nitrogen into the shaker and muddle the herbs.  
3. Add remaining ingredients and shake.  
4. Fine-strain into a chilled Nick & Nora glass.

---

### **Editor’s Notes**

**Bay Leaf Syrup**

* 500 g boiling water  
* 50 g fresh bay leaves  
* White sugar (equal weight to strained liquid)

Pour boiling water over bay leaves and steep 45 minutes. Strain. Weigh the liquid and add an equal weight of sugar. Stir until dissolved. Bottle and refrigerate up to 1 week.

**Saline Solution**

* 200 g distilled water  
* 50 g kosher salt

Blend until fully dissolved.

## **Rosie Martini**

**Ingredients (1 serving):**

* 2 oz gin (e.g., Ford’s)  
* 1 oz dry vermouth (e.g., Method Dry Vermouth)  
* ¾ tsp LoFi Gentian Amaro  
* ¾ tsp cocktail onion brine *(see Editor’s Note)*  
  **Garnish:** expressed lemon twist (discarded), cocktail onion

**Directions:**

1. Add gin, dry vermouth, and gentian amaro to a mixing glass with ice and stir to chill and dilute.  
2. Strain into a glass, then add onion brine and gently stir to incorporate.  
3. Express a lemon twist over the glass and discard it, then garnish with a cocktail onion.

**Editor’s Note – Cocktail Onions:**  
Simmer peeled pearl onions with water, vinegar, salt, sugar, and chile powder, then chill and store in the fridge.

## **Margarita & Cream**

**Ingredients (1 serving):**

* 130 g strawberry-rhubarb ice cream *(see Editor’s Note)*  
* 1 oz blanco tequila (e.g., Patrón)  
* ½ oz lime juice  
  **Garnish:** salted whipped cream

**Directions:**

1. Add ice cream, tequila, and lime juice to a blender and blend until smooth.  
2. Pour into a small glass and top with salted whipped cream. Serve with a spoon.

**Editor’s Note – Strawberry-Rhubarb Ice Cream:**  
Cook strawberry puree and sugar with frozen rhubarb sous vide at 70 °C for 2 hr, blend, freeze in trays overnight, then use as the ice cream base.  
**Salted Whipped Cream:** Beat heavy cream with salt until firm peaks form; best used within one day.

## Tomate o Tomaté

*(Highball, savory mezcal drink — from Frijoleros in Brooklyn)*

**Ingredients (1 serving):**

* 2 oz tomato-infused mezcal *(see notes)*  
* ½ oz bell pepper cordial *(see notes)*  
* ¼ oz Artonic Nectarine liqueur  
* ¼ oz blanc vermouth  
* ¼ oz fino sherry  
* Barspoon absinthe (e.g., Mata Hari)  
* 2 dashes saline solution  
* Soda water, to top  
* Garnish: za’atar salt rim

**Directions:**

1. Rim a highball glass with **za’atar salt**.  
2. Add all ingredients *except* the soda to a shaker with ice and shake.  
3. Strain into the prepared glass over ice and top with soda water.

**Editor’s Notes:**

* *Tomato-Infused Mezcal:* Blend four plum tomatoes with a 750 ml bottle of mezcal.  
* *Bell Pepper Cordial:* Mix 8 oz bell pepper juice with ¼ cup sugar and 1 tsp citric acid; chill.  
* *Za’atar Salt:* Combine equal parts za’atar and salt. 

## **Real Charmer’s Daiquiri**

**Ingredients (1 serving):**

* 1 oz light rum  
* 1 oz fresh lime juice  
* ¾ oz green Chartreuse  
* ¾ oz coconut syrup *(see Editor’s Note)*

**Directions:**

1. Add all ingredients to a shaker with ice and **hard shake**.  
2. **Double-strain** into a chilled Nick & Nora glass.

**Editor’s Note – Coconut Syrup:**  
Blend equal parts coconut cream (e.g., Coconut López) and simple syrup with an immersion blender; refrigerate.

## **Salad Bar Martini**

**Ingredients (1 serving):**

* 1½ oz vodka *(e.g., Bully Boy Seacoast)*  
* ½ oz Moletto Pomodoro Gin  
* ½ oz dry vermouth *(e.g., Dolin)*  
* ½ oz Castelvetrano olive brine  
* ½ oz tomato water *(see Editor’s Note)*  
* ¼ oz manzanilla sherry *(e.g., La Cigarrera)*  
* **Garnish:** cherry tomato

**Directions:**

1. Add all ingredients to a mixing glass with ice and **stir to chill**.  
2. **Strain into a coupe** glass.  
3. Garnish with a cherry tomato.

**Editor’s Note – Tomato Water:**  
Quarter tomatoes, weigh them, and add salt at about 1% of the tomato weight. Blend until smooth, then strain repeatedly through a fine mesh until the liquid runs clear.

## **Archie**

**Ingredients (1 serving):**

* 3 oz *beet-rested mezcal* (see Editor’s Note)  
* ¾ oz fresh lemon juice  
* ½ oz simple syrup (1:1 sugar to water)  
* **Garnish:** lemon twist

**Directions:**

1. Add all ingredients to a shaker with ice and **shake well**.  
2. **Strain over ice** into a glass.  
3. Garnish with a lemon twist.

**Editor’s Note – Beet Mezcal:**  
Cook one small red beet in \~½ inch of water at 375°F (190°C) for \~30 minutes; cool, blend with 750 ml mezcal, strain, and add \~375 ml grapefruit juice (oro blanco or similar). Store chilled.

## **Nashi Highball**

**Ingredients (1 serving):**

* 1¼ oz Japanese whisky (e.g., Suntory Toki)  
* ¾ oz St. George Spiced Pear Liqueur  
* ½ oz manzanilla sherry  
* ¼ oz verjus blanc (e.g., Fusion)  
* ¼ oz pickled ginger syrup *(see Editor’s Note)*  
* ½ tsp cane syrup (2:1 sugar:water)  
* 3 oz soda water  
  **Garnish:** fresh bay leaf

**Directions:**

1. Add all ingredients except soda water to a glass and stir to combine.  
2. Top with soda water.  
3. Garnish with a fresh bay leaf.

**Editor’s Note – Pickled Ginger Syrup:**  
Blend sugar, water, and pickled sushi ginger; strain and refrigerate.

## **Blanka**

**Ingredients (1 serving):**

* 1½ oz gin (such as Fords)  
* 2½ oz apple celery shrub *(see Editor’s Note)*  
* 1 tsp lemon juice  
* 2 dashes absinthe  
* 2 dashes saline solution (8:2 water:salt)  
* 2 dashes white balsamic vinegar

**Directions:**

1. Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice.  
2. “Throw” three times (shake with a bit of flair).  
3. Strain over a large ice cube in a rocks glass.

**Editor’s Note – Apple Celery Shrub:**

* 150 ml Granny Smith apple juice  
* 150 ml celery juice  
* 20 ml ginger juice  
* 25 ml vodka  
* 175 ml simple syrup (6:4 sugar:water)  
* Pinches of citric, ascorbic, and malic acids  
  Juice apples, celery, and ginger; mix with vodka, simple syrup, and acids. Chill 4–5 days.

## **Ni De Aqui Ni De Alla**

**Ingredients (1 serving):**

* 1 oz bourbon  
* 1 oz mezcal (such as Mal Bien)  
* 1 oz plantain syrup *(see Editor’s Note)*  
* 2 dashes Angostura bitters  
* 2 dashes banana bitters  
  **Garnish:** banana leaf

**Directions:**

1. Line a rocks glass with a banana leaf.  
2. Add all ingredients to a mixing glass with ice and stir.  
3. Strain over a large ice cube into the prepared glass.

**Editor’s Note – Plantain Syrup:**

* 1 cup water  
* 1 cup light brown sugar  
* ½ cup sliced ripe plantains  
  Combine water and sugar; heat until sugar dissolves. At \~180 °F, add plantains and simmer 7–10 minutes. Remove from heat, steep 10 minutes, strain, and chill. Keeps up to a week.

## **Frozen Mexican Martini**

**Ingredients (1 serving):**

* 2 oz blanco tequila (preferably LALO)  
* ¾ oz triple sec  
* ½ oz Rose’s Lime Juice  
* ½ oz rich simple syrup (2:1 sugar:water)  
* ¼ oz fresh lime juice  
* 1 oz olive brine  
  **Garnish:** straw, 3 green olives on a skewer, salt rim (optional)

**Directions:**

1. Prepare a stemmed glass with a half salt rim (optional).  
2. Combine all ingredients except olive brine in a blender with ice.  
3. Blend on low for \~5 seconds, then high for \~10 seconds.  
4. Pour into the prepared glass.  
5. Float olive brine on top.  
6. Garnish with a straw and olives.
